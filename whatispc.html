Thus, we are introducing system for checking Plagiarism in assignments.

What is BUSE PLAGIARISM COP?

Buse Plagiarism Cope is a software developed to detect plagiarism on academic assignments which will help to stop copying the assignment of other students and will improve the quality of education at the university and also will help to improve personal skills of students. This software measures the similar text that matches and detects plagiarism for example if 100 assignments have been submitted, each assignment will be compared to the other 99 assignments to check for plagiarism. As well semantical checking will be also done with respect to assignments.
